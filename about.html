About Prophecy App

This is a Prophecy web application where Learners, Teachers, Biblical Scholars, Bible workers share their knowledge about biblical prophecies and their applications

Users can create accounts, log in, and contribute to the community by sharing insights and interpretations of various prophecies.

The app features a user-friendly interface, making it easy for users to navigate and find information.

Prophecy App also includes a discussion forum where users can engage in conversations about different prophecies and their significance.

Whether you're a learner, teacher, biblical scholar, or Bible worker, Prophecy App provides a platform for you to connect with others who share your interest in biblical prophecies.

Daniel and Revelation

  • To understand Biblical prophecies, you need to understand these two books of the Bible
  • First you need to read and understand the book of Daniel
  • Second the book of Revelation
  • Daniel serves as an introduction and background to Revelation.
  • By understanding the historical prophecies and symbolic language in Daniel, one can gain a better understanding of the visions and messages in Revelation.
  • Both books contain apocalyptic literature, which uses symbolic imagery to convey spiritual truths
